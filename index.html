Support & SaaS

In addition to being open source, enterprise support, professional services, and SaaS/PaaS offerings are available to facilitate adoption and deployment.

Enterprise Support

Entprise-class support is available on a per-ticket, hourly or subscription basis.

Professional Services

Professional services are also available at a reasonable fee for enterprise organization that wish to have the platform customized.

SaaS+PaaS

A turnkey hosted Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) version of the Events Tools software s available for a fee. Likewise, managed Platform-as-a-Service (Paas) infrastructure is also available for a fee.
